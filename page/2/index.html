Welcome.

I am an Evolutionary Biologist currently doing a post-doc at the Technical University of Denmark being advised by Dr. Jakob Hemmer Hansen & Dr. Dorte Bekkevold. We belong to the Population Genetics Section, which is located at the small town of Silkeborg in Jutland. This section is part of DTU Aqua, a well-known institute in the field of aquatic resources.
I am hired under the MarGen II Project, and
Previously, I did my MSc and PhD at the Natural History Museum of Denmark — University of Copenhagen under the supervision of Prof. Tom Gilbert & co-advised by Dr. Filipe Vieira.
A morsel about me: I am originally from the Northeast of Brazil, precisely from a city called Natal (it means Christmas in Portuguese). During my two decades living in this tropical town I have the opportunity to visit rather unique plances such as the Atol das Rocas, which is the only atoll in the South Atlantic Ocean — a marvellous place indeed. However, right before I turned 23, I moved to Denmark to do an MSc in Biology. (my favourite anime is Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood).
